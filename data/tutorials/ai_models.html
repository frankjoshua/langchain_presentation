AI Models Currently Available in MonAI

ICBINP

Short for “I can't believe it's not photography”, ICBINP creates stunning photorealistic images. Portraits with perfectly detailed hair, skin and eyes. ICBINP also creates better night and dark images than earlier models.

ICBINP may create nude renders if a clothing style isn't specified. Add “nude” or “naked” to your negative prompts, or specify the clothing to avoid NSFW content.

 

Counterfeit

A beautiful, artsy style, Counterfeit creates amazing anime art and so much more! Counterfeit has a airy, whimsical energy and is capable of creating beautiful artworks with short, easy prompts.

 

DreamShaper v6

DreamShaper creates beautiful fantasy images and portraits in a wide array of styles. Version 6 introduces fantastic photorealism to the DreamShaper brand of beauty and their strong understanding of a wide range of concepts.

 

Dark Sushi

An excellent anime model, Dark Sushi features dark strokes and outlines for a crisp style.

 

Realistic Vision

Realistic Vision creates amazing photorealistic images. Portraits feature detailed hair and skin along with stunning fashions. Realistic Vision also creates impressive landscapes and nature images.

Realistic Vision may render nudes when a clothing style isn't specified, so you might want to add “nude” or “naked” to your negative prompts, or specify the clothing to avoid NSFW content. 

 

Deliberate

Deliberate renders people beautifully, stressing accurate anatomy and skin textures. It can handle detailed prompts with descriptive locations and attire. Deliberate also creates excellent NSFW images, so you may want to add “nude” or “naked” to your negative prompts, or specify the clothing if you want to avoid NSFW content.

Deliberate makes incredible 3D images and highly detailed steampunk renders. Deliberate has an impressive understanding of numerous concepts.

 

DreamShaper v4

DreamShaper v4 creates beautiful anime and digital paintings. 

 

Dreamlike Photoreal

Dreamlike Photoreal delivers photo-quality images in a wide variety of subjects. Great portraits, wildlife, interiors, landscapes, and NSFW images.

 

Anything V4

True to its name, Anything really can do anything, but its real claim to fame is anime. Use “anime” in your prompts for sexier anime results, or use “Japanese animation style” or “magma art” for less erotic results.

 

Dreamlike Diffusion

Dreamlike Diffusion has a wide array of applications, from fantasy images to historical fashions, anime to artistic styles, Dreamlike Diffusion is an excellent go-to AI model.

 

Openjourney

Fine tuned on Midjourney images, Openjourney creates beautiful artistic creations.

 

Stable Diffusion 2.1

SD 2.1 is not the best model for rendering people or faces. However, SD 2.1 is great at capturing the style of historical artistic movements, like Impressionism, Cubism, Fauvism, or recreating the style of certain artists. Other models put their own spin on historical art movements, but SD 2.1 is more faithful to the styles of the time.

 

Inkpunk

Inkpunk renders girls and urban settings in a sketchy style with vibrant colors and gradients. Inkpunk occasionally renders other subjects, but it has a tendency to change a lot of subjects to girls and urban settings.

 

Protogen v3.4

Protogen creates a wide variety of content including detailed sci-fi and fantasy images.

 

Future Diffusion

Future Diffusion creates excellent sci-fi images like spaceships, robots, and futuristic cities.

 

Comic Illustration

Perfect for fantasy illustrations or comic book images, Comic Illustration is good at rendering a complicated scene with several descriptive elements.

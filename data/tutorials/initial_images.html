Using initial images means better quality artwork and easy customizability. Here's how it works.

I want to make a rainbow lion. This is the lion as made by the prompt alone, with no initial image. It has rainbow fur, but I want a different pose.

Unsplash has many beautiful public domain photos which are perfect for initial images. I choose one with good colors, nice composition and no distracting elements. Adding that initial image gives me this image:

At 0.80 the AI is using the colors from the initial image, but not the pose, so I tweak Prompt Strength.

When I dial it down to 0.70, the pose from the initial image is incorporated into the final artwork.
